Rules & Regulations

1)Every team member must belong to a single school i.e no mixed school teams are allowed. However, one school can send multiple teams.

2)Every school from NMIMS Navi Mumbai will compete with each other for the Championship Trophy.

3)For the championship -
a)+10 points will be awarded to every school for participating in any event. The points will be awarded on a per-team basis.
b)+30 points will be awarded to the school for winning a match in any event.
c)-20 points will be deducted from a school if a team is disqualified and the final decision of disqualification will be taken by the Sports Committee.
d)A leader board will be displayed on the campus where schools can check their respective points.
e)In the end, total scores will be added and the school that scores the most points will be awarded the Championship Trophy.

5)Every event has its individual registration link (Google Form). Every player/team must fill out the form only once and attach the payment receipt in the form.

6)The payment details are provided in Google Forms. Also, while making the payment kindly insert the Team name/ Player name in the payment description.
